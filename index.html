    About Us

    Welcome to Oncolinx Cancer Care!

    At Oncolinx Cancer Care, our mission is to provide quality and compassionate care to cancer patients, with a focus on personalized treatment and support. We are dedicated to helping individuals and their families throughout their cancer journey by bridging the gap in cancer care, advancing research and treatment, and enhancing the overall patient experience. Our mission is driven by a deep commitment to improving the lives of those affected by cancer and contributing to advancements in the field of oncology.

    Oncolinx Cancer Care is led by the experienced and compassionate team of Dr. Prashant Chandra Das and Dr. Madhusmita Patro. Dr. Prashant Chandra Das brings 16 years of expertise as a Surgical Oncologist, specializing in robotic and laparoscopic oncosurgery. With a passion for providing optimal outcomes, Dr. Prashant Chandra Das has served patients at renowned institutions, gaining a wealth of knowledge and experience in the field. Dr. Madhusmita Patro, a dedicated healthcare professional, brings her expertise and commitment to cancer care, working tirelessly to support patients and their families throughout their treatment journey.

    At Oncolinx Cancer Care, we provide a comprehensive range of services to address the unique needs of each patient. Our services include expert doctor consultations, where our experienced team works closely with patients to develop personalized treatment plans. We offer state-of-the-art cancer diagnostics, utilizing advanced technologies to accurately diagnose and stage different types of cancer. Additionally, we focus on delivering holistic and supportive care to improve the overall well-being of our patients and their families.

    Our vision at Oncolinx Cancer Care is to be a center of excellence, recognized for our commitment to quality cancer surgery, research, and patient support. We aspire to fill a local and regional need for high-quality cancer care, providing advanced treatments and compassionate services. Through our dedication to advancements in cancer treatment and research, we aim to contribute to the development of innovative therapies and improve outcomes for cancer patients. Our ultimate vision is to empower individuals with hope, healing, and a brighter future in their battle against cancer.
